# Canonical Ruleset of V (vweb)

This ruleset codifies the Magnetar standard for the V implementation.

## Naming Conventions
*   **Directories:** `lower_snake_case` (except root `Vvweb`).
*   **Files:** `snake_case.v`.
*   **Structs:** `PascalCase`.
*   **Functions:** `snake_case`.

## Required Files
*   `README.md`, `PLAN.md`, `BITACORA.md`, `REQUIREMENTS.md`, `ARCHITECTURE.md`, `RULES.md`, `STATUS.md`, `TESTING.md`, `BLOCKERS.md`.
*   `projects/Vvweb.project.yml`.

## Allowed Task States
1.  `planned`
2.  `ready`
3.  `in_progress`
4.  `in_review`
5.  `blocked`
6.  `done`
